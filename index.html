About Us

Welcome to Faisal Saber Editing Resources — the internet's most premium destination for free, high-quality editing assets built specifically for video editors and motion designers.

This platform was created by Faisal Saber, a passionate video editor and content creator who understands exactly what editors need.

What We Offer

Every resource on this platform is carefully curated and tested — from After Effects presets and LUTs to PNG packs, AI prompts, and motion graphics templates. Everything is 100% free.

To give every editor — beginner or pro — access to the same premium tools used in top YouTube edits and AMVs, completely free.
